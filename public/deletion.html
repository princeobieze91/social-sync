Data Deletion Request

Last updated: June 27, 2026

How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of all your data from SocialSync by:

Option 1: Delete from Your Account

Log in to SocialSync and go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will immediately remove all your data including:

  • Your profile and account information
  • All connected social media accounts
  • All posts (drafts, scheduled, and published)
  • Activity logs and analytics data

Option 2: Email Request

Send an email to deletion@socialsync.app with:

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 30 days.

Option 3: Facebook Data Deletion

To also delete data from Facebook:

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings → Apps and Websites
  2. Find "SocialSync" and click Remove
  3. This revokes SocialSync's access to your Facebook data

What Happens

Once your data is deleted:

  • Your account will be permanently deactivated
  • All connected social accounts will be disconnected
  • All content will be permanently removed from our servers
  • The action cannot be undone

Contact

For data deletion questions, email: deletion@socialsync.app
